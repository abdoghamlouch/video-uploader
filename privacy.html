Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal credentials (usernames, passwords). The Extension uses OAuth to obtain access tokens from third-party services (YouTube, TikTok) solely to perform uploads you initiate.

2. How We Use Information

Access tokens are held in Chrome’s secure storage and used only to call the official APIs on your behalf. We do not share or sell these tokens or any usage data.

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts directly with YouTube’s and TikTok’s APIs. Your data is subject to their privacy policies as well.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

The Extension does not use cookies. Any settings or tokens are stored locally in your browser’s extension storage and are not transmitted to our servers.

5. Data Retention

Your OAuth tokens remain stored in your browser until you remove the Extension or revoke access via your Google or TikTok account settings.

6. Security

We follow industry best practices for securing OAuth flows. However, no method is totally secure—use the Extension at your own risk.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact Us

If you have any privacy-related questions, please visit our project page or email us at privacy@example.com.
